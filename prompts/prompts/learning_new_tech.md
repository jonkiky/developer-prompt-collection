## Tips for Better Learning Prompts

1. Be specific about:
   - Your current knowledge level
   - Your learning goals
   - Your time constraints
   - Preferred learning style

2. Ask for:
   - Practical examples
   - Common pitfalls
   - Best practices
   - Real-world applications

3. Follow up with:
   - Clarifying questions
   - Practice exercises
   - Code reviews
   - Implementation feedback
